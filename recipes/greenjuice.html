Green Juice

Description

A refreshing and healthy way to enjoy all sorts of nutrients from fruits and vegetables year round.

Ingredients

  • Kale
  • Spinach
  • Celery
  • Cucumber
  • Orange juice
  • Lime juice
  • Apples

Steps

  1. Wash ingredients, skin and chop into 2-inch chunks.
  2. Toss your cucumbers and celery into a high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Add your remaining ingredients and blend until smooth, about 3 minutes.
  4. Strain the liquid.
  5. Chill until nice and cold, pour into a glass and enjoy!
